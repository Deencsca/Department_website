WELCOME TO DEEN COLLEGE

Deen College of Arts and Science is established by Rafiudeen Educational and Charitable Trust. Dr. A. Rafiudeen is the Chairman. The Commitee Members are Mr. R. Kursheed and Mrs. R. Foujiya Begum. Our College was started on 15/09/2010 with affiliation for 5 courses B.A.,(English)., B.Com., B.B.A., B.Sc.,Computer science., and BCA.

In 2011-2012 affiliation was obtained for B.Sc.,(Chemistry) and B.Sc.,(Maths).
In 2014-2017 affiliation was obtained for MA (English), M.Com, M.Sc (Computer Science).

VISION

To become a Top level, highly acclaimed, innovative and competitive centre of academic and administrative qualities. Excellence in Higher education by offering value added courses through technical and skill based program to the students including minority, backward and rural sections of the society. The motto of the college is "EMPORWER TO EXCELLENCE" invested with values of humanism, patriotism and secularism


MISSION

Provide first grade infrastructure in a green environment that would motivate the students in their pursuit of knowledge. Provide high Quality of Education in all disciplines through a balance of academic and extracurricular programs. Prioritize quality teaching and adopt a variety of approaches and methods in current trends of teaching and learning.
